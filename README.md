1. Precision in Language

Original: “Write a poem.”
Refined Prompt:

Write a 12-line free-verse poem about overcoming failure, using a hopeful and motivational tone.

# 2. Creative Task Expansion

Original: “Describe a futuristic city.”
Refined Prompt:

Describe a futuristic city in the year 2150, focusing on its advanced technology, daily life, cultural values, and social structure.

# 3. Factual Precision

Original: “Tell me about the Eiffel Tower.”
Refined Prompt:

Explain the history, architectural design, and cultural significance of the Eiffel Tower, including when and why it was built.

# 4. Conditional Task Creation

Original: “What would happen if all electricity in the world suddenly stopped?”
Refined Prompt:

What would happen if all electricity in major urban cities suddenly stopped for one week? Focus specifically on the effects on healthcare and transportation systems.

# 5. Summarization and Clarity

Original: “Summarize the book ‘1984’.”
Refined Prompt:

Summarize the novel 1984 from the perspective of Winston Smith, focusing on the theme of government surveillance.

# 6. Comparison and Analysis

Original: “Compare two types of renewable energy.”
Refined Prompt:

Compare solar energy and wind energy in terms of environmental impact, cost efficiency, and technological development.

# 7. Bias Reduction

Original: “Describe the perfect diet.”
Refined Prompt:

Describe the characteristics of a balanced and healthy diet, considering different cultural food traditions, dietary restrictions, and nutritional needs.

# 8. Complex Problem Solving

Original: “Explain how the internet works.”
Refined Prompt:

Explain how data is transmitted across the internet, including the roles of IP addresses, servers, routers, and data packets.

# 9. Relevance and Context

Original: “What are the benefits of exercise?”
Refined Prompt:

What are the physical and mental health benefits of regular exercise for elderly adults?

# 10. Interdisciplinary Prompt Creation

Original: “What impact has social media had on society?”
Refined Prompt:

Analyze the impact of social media on mental health among teenagers, including both positive and negative effects.

# 11. Language Variety and Style

Original: “Describe a beautiful sunset.”
Refined Prompt (Emotional):

Describe a sunset over the ocean using vivid imagery and emotional language that conveys peace and nostalgia.

Refined Prompt (Scientific):

Explain the scientific reasons behind the colors seen during a sunset, including light scattering and atmospheric conditions.

# 12. Cultural Sensitivity and Inclusiveness

Original: “Write about a wedding tradition.”
Refined Prompt:

Describe three different wedding traditions from three different cultures, explaining their meaning and cultural significance.

# 13. Task Framing for Results

Original: “Create a new invention.”
Refined Prompt:

Design an innovative technological device that helps reduce plastic waste in urban areas. Explain how it works and its potential impact.

# 14. Expanding Abstract Concepts

Original: “What is happiness?”
Refined Prompt:

Explain the concept of happiness from both a philosophical perspective (e.g., Aristotle’s ideas) and a psychological perspective (e.g., positive psychology research).

# 15. Tone and Intent

Original: “Explain how to bake a cake.”
Refined Prompt (Casual & Humorous):

Explain how to bake a chocolate cake in a fun, casual, and slightly humorous tone for beginners.

Refined Prompt (Formal):

Provide a detailed, step-by-step formal recipe for baking a chocolate cake, including measurements and baking time.

# 16. Multiple Tasks in One Prompt

Original: “Write a poem about a tree and explain its significance in nature.”
Refined Prompt:

Write a short poem about an oak tree, then explain its ecological importance in supporting wildlife and maintaining soil stability.

# 17. Role-playing and Perspective-Taking

Original: “Describe the life of a king.”
Refined Prompt:

Describe the life of King Louis XIV from a first-person perspective, focusing on his responsibilities and challenges as ruler of France.

# 18. Level of Detail

Original: “Discuss climate change.”
Refined Prompt:

Discuss the causes and effects of climate change in South Asia, and propose three practical solutions for reducing its impact.

# 19. Task Sequencing

Original: “Write a business plan.”
Refined Prompt:

Write a business plan for a startup coffee shop. Structure it in the following order: Executive Summary, Market Analysis, Marketing Strategy, Operations Plan, and Financial Projections.

# 20. Task Ambiguity and Clarity

Original: “Create a website.”
Refined Prompt:

Design a simple e-commerce website for selling handmade jewelry. Describe its homepage layout, product pages, payment system, and essential features such as user login and shopping cart.
